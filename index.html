HOW IT WORKS ??

1. Click on "Start Learning"

Click on start learning button first then it will open new dashboard.

2. Choose a Category

Click on any vibrant category button (Eg ABC,123,COLOUR or FRUITS) from this new dashboard.

3. Sit Back & Learn

Watch and listen as the platform automatically cycles through flashcards.

4. Continue or Pause

Let it run or click stop button to stop the learning.
